ABOUT THE DEV CENTER

WHAT IS THE NASTY DEVELOPER CENTER?

The Nasty Developer Center was created in order to facilitate 3rd party developement of tools and sites. 
We welcome participation and encourage developers to help us continue building new Nasty infrastructure.

Who We Are

NastyFans is a group of Bitcoin enthusiasts that have a lot of fun watching, encouraging, and participating in the Bitcoin world. We as enthusiasts think it is great what NastyMining is doing and we try to help out when possible. In order to coordinate our enthusiasm, we have created NastyFans: The Unofficial Fan Club of NastyMining. We discuss the current and future trends of Bitcoin mining, have polls to vote on ideas for improving NastyMining, and hold fundraisers to help improve NastyMining. As a fan club we do whatever we want to show our enthusiasm for NastyMining. It is a lot of fun!

USD->BTC TOOL

This USD->BTC tool displays an image of a BTC amount converted from a set USD price. You can edit the image foreground and background color, as well as the size, and USD amount being converted.

LIVE IMAGES

Generated mostly using our APIs, these images automatically update to display a range of useful statistics. There are also charts of graphs that can easily be included in your applications. Images update every 15 minutes.
